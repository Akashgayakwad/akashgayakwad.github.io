Aavartan-The Techfest of NIT Raipur

Every year Nitrr conducts a splendiferous Technical Fest Aavartan. Aavartan provides a platform for the curious tech minds to showcase their idea in a worthwhile way. The tech fest fills the atmosphere with the zeal of learning new and the eagerness to create. A splendid mega event VIGYAAN resides in the core heart of Aavartan which is the perfect opportunity of exploiting your tech knowledge and skills in the most better way. Vigyaan promises to nurture the scientific temperament of the students beyond their natural limitations. This scientific rendezvous promises to leave a spine-tingling effect on the participants thereby enhancing tech skills. The technical committee Technocracy also conducts several tech based workshops with the aim of propounding the best of learning and brushing the trending tech concepts. Aavartan, with the Moto of imagine improve implement puts forward the best of opportunities to all the tech geeks thereby quenching the thirst of knowledge and quivering the nerves with the amazing tech concepts.
